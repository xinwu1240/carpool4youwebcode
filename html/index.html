WELCOME TO CARPOOL4YOU




This is a community carpool information sharing website for parents in the San Juan school district, created by a student. Parents can create an account and post requests for carpooling for school-related activities like drop-offs, pick-ups, sports meets, club activities, etc. before or after school. Please only post for these purposes. Be cautious about posting sensitive personal information, as use of this site is at your own risk. The creator of this website is not responsible for the consequences of misuse. Suggestions are welcome at carpool4you@gmail.com. Please allow up to two days for a response. Thank you for your interest!
